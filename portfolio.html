This was the very first serious piece of software that I ever made, it was my final project for my A-levels, and I had a full school year to complete it's development (plus it's corresponding documentation).

It was developed in Python, the only language I knew at the time, and one I had been taught over the previous school year. It uses Pygame, a set of libraries enabling game programming in Python.

Although I see its faults now, at the time it got the highest marks that the school had seen before, a full 100%, which is why I still value it as my first step into software development.
